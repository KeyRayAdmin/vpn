How to Connect

Simple steps to set up your IKEv2 VPN connection on your device.

Step 1: Access Your Device Settings

Open the Settings app on your Android device. Search for "VPN" or find it under "Network & Internet" or "Connections".

Step 2: Add a New VPN Profile

Tap on "VPN" and then select "Add VPN profile". You might need to enter your device's PIN or password.

Step 3: Configure the VPN Settings

Enter the following details:
* **Name:** BlueEdge VPN (or any name you prefer)
* **Type:** IKEv2/IPsec MSCHAPv2
* **Server Address:** (Provided in your credentials, e.g., vpn.blueedge.com)
* **Username:** (Provided in your credentials)
* **Password:** (Provided in your credentials)

Step 4: Connect to the VPN

Save the profile. Then, tap on the BlueEdge VPN profile and enter your username and password again if prompted. Connect to the VPN!

Step 5: Verify Connection

Once connected, you should see a key or VPN icon in your notification bar. You can also check your IP address to confirm your connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about SecureVPN

What is IKEv2 and why is it better than apps? +

IKEv2 is built into Android's system. It's faster, uses less battery, reconnects automatically, and doesn't require any third-party apps or permissions.

How do I set up IKEv2 VPN on Android? +

Go to Settings > VPN > Add VPN Profile > IKEv2/IPSec MSCHAPv2. Enter our server details and credentials. See the guide section for a more detailed walkthrough.

Is there really no device limit? +

Absolutely! Use the same credentials on unlimited devices - phones, tablets, laptops, routers. No restrictions, no additional costs per device.

What if my device doesn't support IKEv2? +

All modern Android devices (6.0+), iOS, Windows, and macOS have native IKEv2 support. For older devices, we can provide alternative configurations.
